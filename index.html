Professional Journey

Welcome to my professional portfolio. My name is Roger McNulty and I am a seasoned Systems Administrator with a strong background in Information Technology and a proven track record of excellence in both private and public sectors. With a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology and extensive hands-on experience, I bring a wealth of technical expertise and a passion for optimizing IT systems to drive organizational success.

My journey began as a dedicated Marine, where I honed my leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving skills. I proudly served two tours in Iraq as a Marine Infantryman. After completing my service, I transitioned to the railroad industry where I worked over a decade as a Train Conductor at Pan Am Railways. There, I developed a keen sense of safety, system optimization, and data analysis.

My passion for technology drove me to pursue a new path. I began working in the Litchfield School District as an IT Support Technician, where I managed IT projects, supported users, and maintained network infrastructure. I then joined BAE Systems as a Systems Administrator I, deploying and maintaining technology solutions in classified lab environments. My responsibilities included configuring server hardware and software, managing inventories, and securing Windows and Linux systems.

My drive for technical excellence and leadership eventually brought me to Raytheon as a Systems Engineer II. I now manage and support both modern and legacy systems. I have recently taken on a major project supporting legacy platforms built on OpenVMS and Fortran, with the goal of becoming the in-house SME. I’m passionate about bridging the gap between legacy and modern systems in defense environments and reconnecting with my programming roots.

My academic path began at Nashua Community College in Software Development, followed by a year at Middlesex Community College where I advanced through courses in Java and C++. I then completed a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at Southern New Hampshire University with a 4.0 GPA and am now pursuing my MBA with a concentration in Project Management.

My technical skills span platforms including Windows, Linux, virtualization technologies, cloud services, and programming languages. Beyond technical proficiency, I bring a calm and analytical approach to problem-solving, a collaborative and team-oriented mindset, and a dedication to exceptional customer service.
